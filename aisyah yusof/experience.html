MY EXPERIENCE

In 2017 after (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia) SPM I started to make my own money using my hand. So I decided to make a product which is 'biscuits'. The products have a variety of biscuits such as Pineapple tart, Chocolate chips, Chocolate rice, Honey cornflakes and Rose biscuit. The product that I sell are freshly from the oven and 100% handmade. The experience that I get in a business is the biscuits are damage in the oven because of over baked.

Beside that my experience working with my mom in processing a Honey bamboo shoots. At the working I need to finding the honey bamboo, cutting it into the small size, packing the item and delivery to the customer. I'm helping my mom to do this work because the job is easy and relax to do. It does not use a lot of energy but need to focus at the work. Plus I can spend my time with my mother more.

A lot of experience that I got during that time. The purpose I working is to know the how the difficulty to get the money, and to spending my time to a beneficial way. Even though my experience is quite short but I enjoying all the bittersweet moments of the time.
